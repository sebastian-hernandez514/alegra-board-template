¿Convertimos el canal contador a freemium? Piloto MX activo — el Board debe avalar el escalamiento March 2026
Context
  • Accountants are the top SMB referral channel in MX and CO — yet current model requires them to pay a $990 MXN/mo plan, creating friction at acquisition.
  • Contalink gaining traction via tax-compliance features (RFC certification). Weak SMB offering but growing brand awareness among accountants.
  • Alegra advantage: full SMB stack (inventory, POS, CRM) that Contalink lacks — but we're not leveraging it as a pull for accountant acquisition.
Proposed Model Shift
  • Current: Accountant pays $990 MXN/mo · SMB freemium with 10 invoices/mo limit.
  • Proposed: Accountant free ($0) · Revenue share ~$10 USD per activated SMB · SMB pays standard Alegra plans.
  • Pilot running in MX since Jan 2026 — 3 cohorts activated, early data in 6 weeks.
Ask to the Board
Endorse pilot scope Validate expanding to 50 accountants in MX before scaling to CO and PE markets.
Revenue share economics Align on $10 USD/activation threshold — is this sustainable given current CAC payback targets?
Risk: cannibalization Assess downside if existing paying accountants migrate to free tier.
